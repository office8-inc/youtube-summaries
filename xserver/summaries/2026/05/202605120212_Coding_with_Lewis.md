### 🎬 導入

This software powers every video that you watch and you probably don't even know about it. FFmpeg or Fastforward Moving Pictures Expert Group, it's a mouthful, is a free and open- source project that has a ton of tools to handle video, audio, and other media related files. It all started in 2000 when a French developer named Fabreze Bolard wrote it as a way to decode and encode video and audio files. And ever since, FFmpeg can convert between almost any media format. It can trim, resize, compress, stream, record, and remix audio and video.
