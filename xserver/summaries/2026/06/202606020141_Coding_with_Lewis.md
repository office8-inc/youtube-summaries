### 📋 背景・概要

But in August of 2023, that quickly changed. Terraform switched to a business source license. So, translation, competitors could no longer use Terraform to build commercial products. Yeah. And almost immediately, a group of developers forked Terraform and launched Open Tofu and put it under the Linux Foundation.

### ⭐ 主要ポイント

And as expected, everyone backed it. So, why? Well, companies were taking Terraform, wrapping it in a managed service, and then selling it back without contributing anything back to the central repository. So, HashiCorp needed to protect their business. But, here's the thing.
